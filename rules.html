Rules

1. Participants must form teams consisting of a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 5 members. If you are solo participant we will group you with some other team.

2. Participants are responsible for bringing their own laptops, chargers, software tools, and any other equipment necessary for development and presentation purposes.

3. Teams must submit their final project or solution within the specified deadline. Late submissions may not be considered for evaluation.

4. All ideas, solutions, and code developed during the Idea-thon must be original and created solely by the participating teams. Plagiarism or use of pre-existing solutions without proper attribution is strictly prohibited.

5. The decisions of the organizing committee and judges are final and binding. Any disputes or issues arising during the Idea-thon will be resolved by the organizing committee
