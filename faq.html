FAQS

Q: Can I participate individually, or do I need to be part of a team?+
Participants must form teams consisting of a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 5 members. If you are solo participant we will group you with some other team.
Q: Will there be any food or refreshments provided during the Idea-thon?+
A: Yes, refreshments will be provided to participants to keep their energy levels high throughout the event. However, participants are advised to plan for their meals accordingly, especially for the overnight sessions
Q: What happens if my team misses the submission deadline?+
A: Late submissions may not be considered for evaluation. It's crucial to adhere to the submission deadline to ensure your project is reviewed by the judges.
Q: How will the winners be determined, and what prizes are available?+
A: Winners will be determined based on the judging criteria, and prizes will be awarded to the top-performing teams. The details of prizes and winner selection will be announced during the closing ceremony of the Idea-thon.
